About

My background is rooted in Classics (BA), Ancient History (MA), Ancient Heritage Studies (PhD), with postdoctoral research in Digital Humanities and extensive teaching in Digital Epigraphy and training in TEI EpiDoc workshops.

My expertise, acquired over a decade of work as a developer, is centred on XML TEI collections of historical documents and literary texts – inscriptions, papyri, manuscripts, early printed texts –, digital lexica, web publishing platforms.
